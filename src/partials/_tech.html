How many students have access to a desktop or laptop computer?
  • Out of 240 districts that responded, 64 said that fewer than 50% of their students had a desktop or laptop computer at home.
    • How many students have access to an internet-enabled phone or tablet?
  • Out of 214 responding districts, 90 districts believed that fewer than 50% of their students had an internet-enabled phone or tablet at home.
    • Responses are from the week of April 12 to 18.
